About us

Moore Developers is a Kenyan based company mainly involved in Web development and Graphic designs. We develop websites for small businesses,large scale businesses and institutions. We do this at affordable prices. The idea of Moore developers was concieved by three university students. The three were pursuing a bachelors degree at the university of Nairobi in the year 2021.
